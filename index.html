Services

We have a wide variety of services that will adapt to your needs.

Consulting services

We are Innova Experts SAS, a company specialized in IT consulting, committed to the digital transformation and technological optimization of companies. We help our clients achieve their business objectives, aligning their technological infrastructure with their strategic needs, ensuring that each solution provides value and improves their operational efficiency.

IT Strategy Consulting

We guide companies in the implementation of technological solutions that drive their growth. We develop strategic plans that connect technology with business objectives, optimizing resources and ensuring that each technological investment is aligned with their long-term vision.

IT Infrastructure and Management

We offer advice on the planning, implementation and management of robust technological infrastructures. Whether through on-premise or cloud solutions, we help our clients design and maintain reliable systems that enable efficient and secure operation.

Custom Software Development

We create custom software solutions that respond to the specific needs of each client. Our team specializes in developing custom applications and platforms that improve internal processes and deliver an exceptional user experience.

Cybersecurity

At Innova Experts SAS we understand the importance of protecting a company's digital assets. We evaluate, design and implement cybersecurity strategies that protect critical information against external threats, ensuring the integrity and confidentiality of data using standards such as ISO 27001 and combining different methods and strategies such as multifactor and Blockchain implementation, so the security of your data becomes our priority.

Data Management and Analytics

We leverage the power of data to generate business value. We design solutions that enable companies to effectively collect, manage, and analyze their data, transforming information into valuable insights that facilitate strategic decision-making.

Frequently Asked Questions

It is natural that you have doubts about our services, so in this space you will be able to find some of those frequently asked questions that could help you clear up those doubts.

1. What consulting services does Innova Experts S.A.S. offer?

We offer consulting in IT strategy, IT infrastructure and management, custom software development, cybersecurity, data management and analytics, cloud computing services, and technical support and maintenance.

2. How can I hire the consulting services of Innova Experts S.A.S.?

You can contact us directly through our website or by email. One of our advisors will contact you to analyze your needs and offer you the best solution.

3. What types of payment plans do you offer?

We offer three payment plans Basic: Ideal for small phases of projects or specific services. Entrepreneur: Designed for entrepreneurs looking to grow without having previous experience. Premium: A complete package for large companies, such as multinationals, that require more comprehensive solutions.

4. What does IT Strategy consulting consist of?

We guide companies in the implementation of technological solutions aligned with their business objectives, maximizing the use of resources and ensuring that each technological investment has a positive impact.

5. How do you help with IT infrastructure management?

We advise on the planning, implementation and management of robust technological infrastructures, both on-premise and in the cloud, ensuring that systems are secure and efficient.

6. Do you offer custom software development services?

Yes, we develop custom software solutions that respond to the specific needs of each client, optimizing their processes and improving the user experience.

7. What cybersecurity solutions do you offer?

We evaluate, design and implement cybersecurity strategies to protect companies' critical information from external threats.

8. How can you help me manage my data?

We offer solutions to collect, manage and analyze data effectively, transforming this information into insights that facilitate strategic decision-making.

9. What services do you offer related to cloud computing?

We help companies migrate to the cloud, offering advice on the adoption and optimization of cloud infrastructures, ensuring a smooth transition.

10. Do you have technical support and maintenance?

Yes, we provide ongoing technical support and preventative maintenance to ensure that our clients' technological infrastructure operates optimally.
